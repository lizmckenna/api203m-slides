Plausible. Thematic. But these are summaries, not discoveries. The LLM named what is already visible on the surface. “Gendered Technology Access” is technically correct. But it misses that she is talking about her partner, her household, her intimate life as the site where differentiation is happening. A proponent would say: better prompting fixes this. “Flag where speakers locate anxiety in specific relationships rather than abstract peer comparisons.” Fair.
